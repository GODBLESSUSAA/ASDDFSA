Zoopia is an innovative platform dedicated to bitcoin ecosystem staking, providing a secure, efficient, and captivating staking service for cryptocurrency holders. our vision is to create a vibrant digital zoo where users stake their brc20 tokens, earning stable and substantial returns while participating in an automatic compounding process.

Vision

Zoopia envisions a thriving ecosystem where users harness the power of their BRC20 tokens by securely staking them, earning rewards, and participating in auto-compounding processes. We aim to empower users with secure and efficient staking while fostering a digital zoo where every token flourishes.

Key Features

Non-Custodial Staking:

Users retain control of their tokens while staking, ensuring security and autonomy over their assets.

BRC20/ARC20 Support:

Zoopia accommodates a wide range of BRC20/ARC20 tokens, allowing users to stake various tokens within the ecosystem.

Auto-Compounding:

Effortlessly grow your staked assets through automatic reinvestment of rewards, compounding your earnings over time.

Diverse Staking Options:

Providing various staking choices, including different durations, reward modes, growth mechanisms, and dynamic interest rate mechanisms adjusted in real-time according to market changes, catering to diverse user needs.

Community Governance:

Implementing decentralized community governance, allowing token holders to participate in platform decision-making, and enhancing community involvement and ownership.
